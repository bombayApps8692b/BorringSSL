❖ Reset Pin/Forgot Pin

Steps to resolution:-


Solution 1: - Steps for RESET PIN

Step 1: - Start the MOSAMBEE PAYMENTS application and click on Reset pin.
Step 2: -A new window will open; enter your registered mobile number.
Step 3: You will receive 4 digit OTP on your registered number via SMS.
Step 4: - Start the Mosambee Payments application and enter your registed mobile number and OTP that you have received via SMS.
Step 5: -You will be forced to choose your 4 digit PIN, confirm your 4 digit PIN and login with the new PIN.

❖ Pairing Related Question

Steps to resolution:-

Solution 1: - Steps for pairing MOSAMBEE mPos device and Mobile phone

Step1: - Go to smart phone menu and click on Settings.
Step 2: - Click on Wireless and network.
Step 3: - Click on Bluetooth to switch on the bluetooth.
Step 4: - Click on Bluetooth settings.
Step 5: - Start Mosambee mPos device by pressing ON/OFF button from right side.
Step 6: - Select QPOSXXXXXXXX (Device Name).
Step 7: - Click on Device name for pairing on your mobile phone.
Step 9: - The device will be paired.
